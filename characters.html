Garadyn Montauk

Race: Human

Age: 38

Garadyn is the current head of AFPA, and is situated in the main HQ in Fairport. He moves around occasionally to take the more difficult jobs in different areas, but does not hunt as much -- much to his dismay -- due to his position requiring him to manage others more. He is a skilled and powerful Dread Hunter who earned his place at the top of AFPA through sheer skill and dedication, and even in his age is still one of the most powerful Dread Hunters currently alive.
